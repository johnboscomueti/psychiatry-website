Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of a typical therapy session?

The duration of a therapy session can vary depending on the individual and the treatment plan. Generally, a therapy session can last between 45 to 60 minutes.

Do I need a referral from my primary care physician to see a psychiatrist?

In most cases, a referral from a primary care physician is not necessary to see a psychiatrist. You can directly schedule an appointment with a psychiatrist.

What types of conditions do you treat?

We provide comprehensive care for a wide range of mental health conditions, including anxiety disorders, depression, bipolar disorder, OCD, PTSD, and more. Our team of experts will evaluate your specific needs and develop a personalized treatment plan.
